Aura Check

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

Alpha Intel Labs ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Aura Check mobile application and website (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you sign in with Google or Apple, your name and profile information provided by those services.

Photos

Aura Check uses your device camera or photo library to capture outfit images. These photos are sent to our servers and to Google's Gemini AI service for analysis. Photos are processed in real time and are not permanently stored on our servers. They are discarded after the AI analysis is complete.

Device Information

We collect a device identifier to manage free trial limits. This identifier is generated locally and does not contain personally identifiable hardware information.

Usage Data

We collect basic usage information such as the number of scans performed, subscription tier, and feature usage to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service: Process your photos through AI analysis, generate outfit critiques and virtual try-on images, and deliver style chat responses.
  • Account Management: Authenticate your identity, manage subscription status, and enforce scan limits.
  • Improve the Service: Understand usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features.
  • Communications: Send password reset emails and essential account notifications. We do not send marketing emails.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database (Firestore), cloud functions, and web hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI — Photo analysis and image generation. Photos you submit are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. Google AI Terms
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management and payment processing (for paid tiers). RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple / Google — If you purchase a subscription through the App Store or Google Play, your payment is processed by Apple or Google respectively. We do not have access to your payment card details.

4. Data Retention

  • Account data (email, subscription tier, scan count) is retained in our database until you delete your account.
  • Photos are processed ephemerally — they are sent to the AI for analysis and discarded. They are not stored on our servers after processing.
  • Local data (scan history, reference photos, cached results) is stored on your device and can be cleared from the Settings screen in the app.

5. Your Rights

  • Delete your account: You can delete your account and all associated data from the Settings screen in the app. This permanently removes your user profile, scan history, and device links from our servers.
  • Data export: You may request a copy of your data by emailing us.
  • Opt out: You can stop using the Service at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase security rules that restrict data access to authenticated users, and server-side authentication for all cloud functions. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Aura Check is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the effective date. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
